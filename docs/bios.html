Get to know the Band

Harvey Cement is the fiery lead vocalist of the band, known for his intense stage presence and unapologetic energy. Hailing from Austin, Texas, Harvey’s voice echoes the raw passion of the punk scene, with lyrics that tackle everything from personal struggles to societal unrest. His rebellious spirit and unfiltered attitude make him the perfect frontman, always ready to rally the crowd with his bold and gritty performance. When he's not belting out anthems on stage, Harvey can be found championing local causes and diving into the city’s thriving underground art scene.

Dale Cement brings the heavy, infectious riffs that drive the band's signature sound. With his roots deeply planted in the Austin music scene, Dale channels the city's eclectic energy into every note he plays. His mastery of both classic punk and modern influences gives the band a distinct edge, while his aggressive guitar playing and intricate solos keep fans hooked from start to finish. Offstage, Dale is an avid collector of vinyl records and loves exploring Austin’s record stores for hidden gems from the punk archives

Connor Cement is the powerhouse drummer behind the relentless rhythm section of the band, known for his thunderous beats and tight precision. With a love for fast tempos and heavy drum fills, Connor brings a level of intensity that propels the band's music forward, making every song feel like an adrenaline rush. Raised in Austin, he grew up immersed in the city’s diverse punk and hardcore scene, shaping his powerful, no-holds-barred drumming style. Outside of music, Connor is passionate about photography and often captures candid moments from the local punk community.
